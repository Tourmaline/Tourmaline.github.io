Welcome!

I am a PhD student at the Division of Scientific Computing at Uppsala University. I am developing methods for large scale electronic structure calculations, check details in the research page.

My supervisors are Emanuel Rubensson and Elias Rudberg.




Research interests

Electronic structure calculations

  • Numerical methods and software for large-scale electronic structure calculations.
  • Linear scaling techniques to compute the density matrix, in particular recursive polynomial expansions.

Numerical linear algebra

  • Large sparse eigenvalue problems arising in linear scaling electronic structure calculations.

High performance computing

  • Efficient parallelization of the electronic structure software on distributed memory clusters.
